Hi! I'm Ed Sperr. I'm the Systems and Discovery Librarian at the [University of Georgia Libraries](https://www.libs.uga.edu/). There I support users' ability to identify, retrieve, and use library resources by helping to maintain the Libraries' Alma ILS and other systems. In my previous role I spent eight years providing point-of-care information and research support to residents in the AU/UGA Internal Medicine Residency program. 

My development practice is centered on new ways to visualize and interact with bibliographic data. I have also spent several years teaching improv to to a wide variety of audiences and am a member of the [Medical Improv Collaborative](https://medicalimprovcollaborative.org/). 

Feel free to look at my [CV](/assets/Sperr CV.pdf).
